what is Structural Directive

In Angular, a Structural Directive is a type of directive that changes the structure of the DOM (Document Object Model) by adding or removing elements based on specific conditions. These directives are responsible for modifying the layout or structure of the view dynamically.

1. ngIf: Conditionally includes or removes an element from the DOM based on a boolean expression.
